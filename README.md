# COFFEE
### The Artometrics of a Global Commodity

A data report analyzing the global coffee market — production volumes, trade flows, and price dynamics across the world's most traded agricultural commodity. Published at [artometrics.com](https://artometrics.com).

## What's in this report

- **Global production by country** — how Brazil, Vietnam, and Colombia dominate supply and how the geographic concentration of production creates structural price risk
- **Export and trade flows** — where coffee moves after harvest and how the gap between producing and consuming nations shapes the economics of the supply chain
- **Price dynamics** — the relationship between production cycles, weather events, and commodity price volatility

## Data source

TidyTuesday Coffee dataset loaded directly from the [TidyTuesday GitHub repository](https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday). Original data sourced from the International Coffee Organization (ICO).
